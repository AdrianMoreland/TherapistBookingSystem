About me


Hi! I'm Jane,
a psychology therapist with extensive
experience in various therapeutic areas.
My focus lies in psychotherapy, couples therapy,
and sexology, where I'm dedicated to
helping individuals and couples explore
the intricate aspects of the human mind
and relationships.

You can find my practice conveniently located at 81 Newmarket Square in the heart of Dublin,
where I currently offer in-person
therapy sessions.


My primary focus lies in fostering healthier and more fulfilling relationships. I specialize in relationship therapy, sexology, and couples therapy because I firmly believe that the quality of our relationships profoundly impacts our overall well-being. I understand that relationships can be both a source of immense joy and a significant challenge. That's why I'm here to provide the guidance and support needed to enhance communication, resolve conflicts, and deepen the emotional and physical intimacy between partners. Through my work, I aim to empower individuals and couples with the tools and insights necessary to build more satisfying and harmonious relationships.

Your journey towards healthier and happier relationships begins here.

I'm actively working on expanding my services to include online therapy sessions. One of my goals is to make therapy more accessible and convenient for my clients.


I believe in providing quality therapy services that are accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.
Currently, my standard pricing is set at 60 euros per hour for individual therapy sessions and 100 euros per hour for couples therapy, ensuring that clients receive the dedicated care and support they deserve.

I'm also acutely aware of the challenges that many individuals and couples face, particularly when it comes to managing their mental health during difficult financial times. It's my genuine belief that everyone should have the opportunity to find wellness and healing, even when facing financial difficulties.
That's why I'm dedicated to working with those in need to offer more affordable rates, tailored to their specific circumstances.
